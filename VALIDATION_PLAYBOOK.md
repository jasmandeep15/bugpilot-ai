# BugPilot AI Validation Playbook

## 30-Day Goal

Validate whether small SaaS teams will pay for better support-to-engineering handoff.

Success criteria:

- 30 people contacted
- 10 useful replies
- 3 demo users
- 1 strong payment signal

## Target Buyers

- SaaS founders
- Engineering managers
- Support leads
- Dev agencies maintaining client software
- Indie hackers with active products

Avoid enterprise buyers at this stage. They require security reviews, procurement, SSO, compliance, and longer sales cycles.

## Outreach Message

```text
Hi [Name], I’m building BugPilot AI for small software teams.

It turns messy support tickets, logs, and Slack/Jira notes into clean engineer-ready bug reports, reproduction steps, RCA drafts, and customer replies.

Curious: does your team lose time converting customer issues into actionable dev tickets?
```

## Demo Follow-Up

```text
I can send a 2-minute demo using a fake SaaS bug. If it looks useful, I’d love your feedback.
```

## Discovery Questions

- How do customer issues currently become engineering tickets?
- Who writes the first bug report: support, founder, QA, or engineer?
- What makes a bug report painful for engineers?
- How often do tickets get sent back because information is missing?
- How do you communicate updates back to customers?
- What tool do you use today: GitHub, Jira, Linear, Notion, Slack, Zendesk, Intercom?
- Would better handoff quality save time or reduce customer frustration?
- What would make this worth paying for?

## Demo Script

1. Open BugPilot AI.
2. Select the payment failure sample.
3. Show the messy input.
4. Show generated report:
   - Priority
   - Confidence
   - Evidence quality
   - Reproduction steps
   - Missing information
   - Developer handoff
   - Customer reply
5. Export to GitHub/Jira/Linear format.
6. Ask: “Would this save time in your current workflow?”

## Pricing Test

Start with:

- $19/month for solo founder
- $49/month for small team
- $99/month for team with custom templates

Ask:

```text
If this connected to your current issue tracker and saved your team 2-3 hours per week, would $49/month be reasonable?
```

## Pivot Rules

Continue if buyers say:

- Support-to-engineering handoff is painful.
- Missing context causes delays.
- Customer updates are stressful or inconsistent.
- They want GitHub/Jira/Linear export.
- They would pay if accuracy and privacy are handled.

Pivot if buyers say:

- Existing tools solve this well enough.
- They do not have enough support volume.
- They like the demo but would not pay.
- They distrust generated reports even with human review.

## Likely Pivots

- RCA-only tool for engineering managers.
- Support-to-Jira automation for SaaS support teams.
- AI runbook generator from incidents.
- Compliance/security questionnaire assistant for startups.
